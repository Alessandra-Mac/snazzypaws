Frequently Asked Questions

You can book online through our booking page, call us, or visit us in person during opening hours.

We require all dogs to be up-to-date with their core vaccinations, including parvovirus, distemper, and kennel cough. Please bring proof of vaccination to your first appointment.

Most appointments take between 1–3 hours, depending on the size, breed, and services required. We will give you an estimated pick-up time when you drop off your pet.

For safety and to help your dog relax, we ask that owners do not stay in the grooming area. You are welcome to wait in our reception area or return at pick-up time.
